General

Cost of entry

Adults : $25, Children : $10

What happens if I lose my belongings?

Please go to the nearest customer service counter for the Lost and Found.

How to get there

There are various modes of transportation to Kingdoom Carnival.

Guests can travel by bus, car, foot and train.

Bus

173, 369, 548

Train

Rules & Regulation

Age requirement for rides

12 and above

Height requirement for rides

1.30 cm and above

For the disabled or pregnant?

Unfortunately, we ask that you refrain from getting onto rides due to safety precaution

Ensure everyone has the best experience

You are advised to wear comfortable clothes and shoes. Please be properly clothed at all times. No costumes or full-face masks are allowed inside the attractions.
